Who we are

Arion Tech Sol is Ahmedabad based societal startup, Founded in December 2016 with a clear focus fulled by passion on preserving the most valuable resource- WATER.

For a developing country like India, water remains a resource beyond the reach of many, especially the rural sectors. Arion Techsol believes in connecting the scattered dots of cost effectiveness, technology, and renewable energy sources to deliver effective and efficient water management solutions.
